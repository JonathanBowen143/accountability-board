Commonwealth Accountability Board

The Commonwealth of Massachusetts Justice System Accountability Board began as a historical investigation and outgrew its scope. The past and present share the same nodes — a map of the injustice of justice that has wracked victims across this Commonwealth since 1854. Karen Read, Juston Root, Sandra Birchmore, and John O’Keefe: each case traces back through the same institutions, the same people, the same decisions protected by the same power. The accountability board rewards exploration — any node pulls loose; any cluster isolates on hover. Jonathan Bowen built this map; suggestions can be sent to @BostonByBirth on X.
