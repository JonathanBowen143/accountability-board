# Recent Overturned Murder Conviction Mining

Purpose: preserve the names and source trail from the June 2026 mining pass without forcing every contested or overturned conviction onto the public board.

Public-board rule for this lane: add a person or institution only when the source supports a direct role in a wrongful-conviction, overturned-conviction, or contested-prosecution pattern. Exonerees and wrongfully convicted people stay as small gold-ring nodes unless the project opens an active murdered-person accountability lane around that person.
