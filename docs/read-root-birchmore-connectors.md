# Read / O'Keefe / Root / Birchmore Connector Notes

Last updated: June 9, 2026.

This note documents the connector pass added after the Karen Read witness ledger. It is not a claim that every named person belongs in the graph. The board should stay selective: add direct graph edges when the public record shows a meaningful case, institutional, professional, personal, or filing-based relationship.

## Added graph connectors

John O'Keefe now appears as a person node as well as the larger O'Keefe / Read case node. The personal node makes the human connection readable when spotlighting Karen Read. Added edges connect O'Keefe to Karen Read, the Boston Police Department, the O'Keefe case node, Brian Albert, Jennifer McCabe, and Kerry Roberts.

Karen Read now has direct case edges to Yuriy Bukhenik, Brian Tully, John Fanning, David DiCicco, Sean Goode, and Adam Lally. These edges are intended to make her visible neighborhood match the public trial and misconduct record, not to imply the same level of personal relationship for every node.

The board now has a distinct `court filing / discovery` edge. It is used for Karen Read / Sandra Birchmore and O'Keefe case / Birchmore links because the strongest public source is procedural: Read's defense sought unredacted Birchmore investigation records and argued that material witnesses and misconduct allegations overlapped.

The Office of Chief Medical Examiner now has direct case edges to O'Keefe / Read, Birchmore, and Root. The board already had individual OCME-linked doctors, but the direct institutional edges make the cross-case pattern visible without adding new people.

## Source basis

CBS Boston reported that Read's attorneys sought unredacted documents from the Sandra Birchmore death investigation. The article also reports the defense filing's claim that the Birchmore investigation involved many material witnesses in Read's case, and it names Morrissey, Michael Lank, Kevin Albert, Brian Tully, and John Fanning as public officials or officers the filing described as involved in both cases.

Scripps/AP reported that Brian Tully and Yuri Bukhenik, along with Michael Proctor, faced internal-affairs investigation after the Read trial, and that there was insufficient evidence to prove allegations against John Fanning. The same piece describes defense concerns about personal relationships, sloppy police work, and the handling of the O'Keefe scene.

WCVB reported that several Massachusetts State Police figures linked to the Read trials were also listed as potential witnesses in the Brian Walshe case: Connor Keefe, John Fanning, Andre Porto, Yuri Bukhenik, Brian Tully, Michael Proctor, and David DiCicco. That supports keeping the Read investigator links visible because they recur outside the O'Keefe case.

Vanity Fair tied the public controversy around the Read, Birchmore, and Root cases to Norfolk County DA Michael Morrissey's office. It also reported that Proctor and Bukhenik were involved in the Root investigation and that Root family member Jennifer Root Bannon viewed the Read and Birchmore cases as part of a broader transparency/accountability pattern.

NBC10 Boston's Commonwealth Confidential reporting describes the Root shooting, Norfolk DA review, the Boston-to-Brookline chase, the state-police involvement, and the broader conflict-of-interest concern when district attorneys investigate state police in Massachusetts.

The DOJ detention affidavit in United States v. Matthew Farwell alleges that Matthew Farwell killed Sandra Birchmore to prevent disclosures to law enforcement, describes the Stoughton Police Explorers connection, and states that Birchmore's body was transported to the Office of Chief Medical Examiner, which ruled the manner of death suicide in 2021.

AP reporting identifies John O'Keefe as Karen Read's Boston police officer boyfriend and describes the prosecution, acquittal on major charges, and Read's later lawsuit against the Massachusetts State Police and Canton.

Court TV and ABC News support the O'Keefe personal-neighborhood edges: Court TV describes the Waterfall/Fairview circle, Brian Albert's home, and Jennifer McCabe's role; ABC News describes Kerry Roberts as O'Keefe's lifelong friend and a key witness.

## Open connector leads

The next pass should verify whether Michael Lank, Kevin Albert, Nicholas Guarino, and Brian Tully deserve additional direct Birchmore edges from official records rather than relying only on Read defense filings or secondary reporting.

The Root report should be extracted from the official Norfolk DA PDF, if available, before adding more officer-level Root connectors beyond the already documented Proctor, Bukhenik, Tully, DiCicco, Guarino, and shooting-scene officer links.

The graph should not add every Karen Read witness. The witness ledger remains the holding place for names that are documented but do not yet have a meaningful accountability connector.
